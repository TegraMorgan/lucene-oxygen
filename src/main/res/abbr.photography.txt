(<a href="/wiki/CMYKOG" class="mw-redirect" title="CMYKOG">CMYKOG</a>) It is possible to omit black ink, in which case the process is termed CMY.<sup id="cite_ref-hamber_9-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-hamber-9">[9]</a></sup></td>
